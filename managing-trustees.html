Managing Trustees

K K Raja, Founder

KK Raja, the founder and the source of inspiration behind the Latha Raja Foundation, is a Civil Engineer, promoter of Indian Art and Culture, Event Manager and above all a hope of the hopeless. He was the Founder Secretary of Chaitanya Art Theatres. His vision and mission and eternal love for art and culture made the Chaitanya Art Theatres as one of the leading cultural organizations in Telangana and AP with branches in Vijayawada and Vishakapatnam. He organised 1700 events under the banner of Chaitanya Art Theatres for the past 30 years which include memorable events like 10-day 'Magicania', a Magic and Musc Festival, 31-day cultural festival of 'Nelaninda Vennala' and 'Kala Kiranalu-20', a cultural fest where 20 cultural programmes run at same time on same day in 20 theatres in Hyderabad. For the past 8 years, through Exotic Events & Promotions Pvt Ltd., an one stop resource for total Event Management solutionm Mr. Raja had organised many notable and praiseworthy events like 'Octave', a 3-day northeastern state folk festival. He had worked as an event manager for several State Governament events in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh for Department of Culture, Language, Agriculture etc., where dignitaries like Chief Ministers of the State, Governors, Central Ministers and State Ministers attended as honorable guests.

N Siddoji Rao, International Ford Fellow (USA)

Dr. N. Siddoji Rao had his studies at JNU, New Delhi, Harvard University(USA), Australian National University. He was a International Ford Fellow(USA), Australian Government Fellow and ICSSR Fellow(India). He worked in Australia, Indonesia(at World Bank, Jakartha) and India(Former Academic Director of AP Study Circle and Associate Fellow of ICSSR Journal). He is also Ford International Leadership Trainee. Presently, He is the Secretary, Help Desk, a Charitable Organization.

B Anil Kumar, MD, Ashwini Group

Anil Kumar did his PG BBM(Marketing & Finance) at Bhavan’s College, Hyderabad. He is the Managing Director, Aswini Group of Companies. He is the Vice President and a Trustee, Bharat Vikas Parishad. He is the General Secetary, Laghu Udyog Bharathi and the Chairman, Bharat Vikas Pairishad Generic Medical Shops, Telangana.

K Raja Anjana,Director, Exotic Events

Raja Anjana did her B.E. at CBIT, Hyderabad and MBA from IIM, Sambalpur. She has authored Inked Dewdrops, a micro-poetry book and a few short stories. She is a Director at Exotic Events and Promotions Pvt Ltd.

K Raja Sanjana,Director, Exotic Events

K. Raja Sanjana did her B.E. at CBIT, Hyderabad and pursuing M. Des at Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology, Banglore.
