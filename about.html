About Us

The Latha Raja Foundation was established in the year of 2015 in order to honour the memory of Smt. K. S. S. Latha, Director, Exotic Events.

The foundation focuses on providing financial aid for educational requirements of students in need, acts as the platform for talented artists to promote and showcase a variety of lesser-known art forms. A lot of importance has been given to create awareness about a broad area of socially relevant content by publishing and distributing books for free. We have extended our support to other organizations which work towards the welfare of the society. We also recognize the effects of such organizations at the national and state levels through the Seva Dharmik Awards.

The foundation has recently acquired its name but was giving back to society for the last thirty years under the title of “Chaitanya Art Theaters” - a social and cultural organization.

The objectives of the trust are:

1

To render free medical aid to the poor and the deserving. Establish free dispensaries and distribute medicines to the needy at free of cost.

2

To institute and award periodical scholarships, grant aid to Indian Citizens for study and research in the field of education, Indian Culture and Fine Arts, to make the contribution for the relief of poor, aged and handicapped artists or students in the fields of education, Indian Culture and Fine Arts.

3

To publish, print and distribute or sell gratuitously with no-profit motive., Journals, Books, Periodicals etc that the trust may think desirable for the promotion of its objectives.

4

To do social service in the areas of Slum Development, Community Development, Education Development, Medial Aid, Care of the Aged, Self-Employment Scheme and other out-reach activities.

5

To incorporate or to grant affiliation to institutions at home or abroad having objects wholly or in part similar to the trust.
